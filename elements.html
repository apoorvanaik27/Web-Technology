Basketball Dog

From director Vicki Fleming comes the heartwarming tale of a boy named Pete and his dog Rover. You may think a boy and his dog learning the true value of friendship sounds familiar, but a big twist sets this flick apart: Rover plays basketball, and he's doggone good at it.

This movie has everything you could ask for:

  • Basketball
  • A dog
  • Nail-biting suspense
